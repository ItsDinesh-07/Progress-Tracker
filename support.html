Frequently Asked Questions
Click any question to expand the answer
Your progress is automatically saved to your device's local storage as you update your tasks and goals inside the tracker. There's no need to manually save — every mark you complete or skip is stored instantly.
Click the "New Progress" button in the top navbar or the "Create Progress" button on the home page. Enter a name, set a start and end date, and your tracker will be ready to use immediately.
The Analytics page gives you a full performance overview — including completion rate over time, consistency heatmap, goal progress list, streak tracker, and smart insights. It reads all your progress data and visualises it with charts and meters.
A streak is a consecutive chain of days where you've marked your progress as "complete." Missing even one day resets the streak. Your current streak and best-ever streak are both tracked and shown in the Analytics section.
Yes! Click the three-dot menu (⋮) on any progress card and select "Pin to Top." Pinned progress will always appear at the top of your list with a yellow pin badge. You can unpin it the same way anytime.
All your data is stored entirely on your device using browser localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server — your progress, history, and notifications are fully private and only accessible on your own device.
Deleted progress is moved to your History log with a "Deleted" tag and the date it was removed. You can view your full deletion history through the History panel in the sidebar. The history can be cleared separately from the History popup.
Achievements are milestones you unlock by reaching goals — like completing your first tracker, hitting a 7-day streak, or finishing 100 productive days. Each achievement rewards XP which increases your level shown in the Achievements popup.
Completed means 100% of days are marked done. In Progress means it's currently active within its date range. Expired means the end date has passed without completion. Not Started means the start date hasn't arrived yet.
Currently, data is stored locally on each device. Cross-device sync with cloud backup is planned for a future update. For now, your progress on one device won't automatically appear on another device.
Notifications in Progress Tracker are in-app alerts — they appear in the bell icon popup in the navbar. You'll get alerts when a progress starts today, when you're 3 days from a deadline, or when you complete a goal at 100%. Make sure you visit the app on that day to trigger them.
The app currently uses a basic login check to protect your home screen. Full authentication with account creation and cloud profiles is planned for a future update.
