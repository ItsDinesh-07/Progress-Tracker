Progress Tracker is built with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

All your data stays on your device. Progress records, history, notifications, and settings are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device.

No personal data is collected by Progress Tracker servers.
No cookies are used for tracking or advertising purposes.
No third-party analytics or tracking scripts are embedded in the application.
Clearing your browser's localStorage will permanently delete all your Progress Tracker data.

Since all data is stored locally, Progress Tracker cannot recover your data if it is deleted, if you clear your browser storage, or if you switch to a different device or browser.
