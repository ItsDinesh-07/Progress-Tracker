Section 02
How We Use Your Information

All data stored in your browser is used exclusively to provide and improve your experience within Progress Tracker. Specifically:

  • Progress tracking: To display your goals, daily completion statuses, streaks, and analytics.
  • App functionality: To remember your pinned progress, favorites, filter preferences, and notification states across sessions.
  • Performance insights: To calculate your consistency rate, best streak, weekly comparisons, and achievement progress.
  • Notifications: To generate in-app alerts (e.g., deadline reminders, streak alerts) based on your progress data — all computed locally.
  • Search experience: To remember your recent search keywords so you can quickly access past searches.
None of your usage data is transmitted to any external server for behavioral profiling, advertising, or resale. All computations happen entirely in your browser.
Section 03
Local Storage & Data Storage

Progress Tracker uses your browser's localStorage API to persist your data between sessions. This is similar to how a desktop application saves files on your computer — except it lives in your browser.

The following keys are stored in your browser's localStorage:

  • allProgress — Your complete list of progress goals and daily entries.
  • pinnedProgress / favoriteProgress — IDs of goals you've pinned or starred.
  • deletedHistory — A log of progress goals you have deleted (kept for the History view).
  • recentlyOpened — The last 5 progress goals you opened.
  • progressNotifs — In-app notification objects.
  • recentSearches — Up to 5 recent search keywords.
  • loggedIn — A simple flag ("true") indicating you are logged in.
  • notifiedStartIds / notifiedCompleteIds / notifiedDeadlineIds — IDs used to avoid duplicate notifications.
Important: Clearing your browser's site data or localStorage will permanently delete all your progress data. We recommend exporting or backing up your data regularly if this concerns you. This data is not recoverable once cleared.

Your localStorage data is isolated to the domain where Progress Tracker is hosted. No other website can access it.

Section 05
Cookies & Tracking

Progress Tracker does not use browser cookies (document.cookie) for any purpose. We rely solely on localStorage for data persistence.

  • No advertising cookies are set.
  • No tracking pixels or beacon scripts are used.
  • No fingerprinting techniques are employed.
  • No cross-site tracking occurs.
You will never see a cookie consent banner on Progress Tracker because we simply do not use tracking cookies. Your browsing activity within the app stays entirely on your device.
Section 06
Data Security

Because Progress Tracker operates entirely client-side, your data security is primarily governed by your own device and browser security. We implement the following best practices:

  • HTTPS enforcement: The application is served over HTTPS to prevent man-in-the-middle attacks during page load.
  • No server-side storage: Since your data never leaves your browser in the current version, there is no server-side database to be breached.
  • No external authentication: Login state is managed via a simple localStorage flag. We recommend only using Progress Tracker on trusted personal devices.
  • Script integrity: External scripts are loaded from reputable, widely-trusted CDNs.
Your responsibility: Do not use Progress Tracker on shared or public computers where others may access your browser's localStorage. Always log out when using a shared device to protect your data.

While we take reasonable steps to secure the application code, no software system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage you to keep your browser and operating system updated.

Section 07
Your Rights & Choices

You have complete control over your data within Progress Tracker. Here is what you can do at any time:

  • Access your data: All your progress data is visible within the app. You can view any goal, day entry, or note at any time.
  • Delete individual progress: Use the Delete option on any progress card to remove it permanently. Deleted items move to your History view.
  • Clear all data: Open your browser's Developer Tools → Application → Local Storage → clear all keys related to Progress Tracker to wipe everything.
  • Clear history: Use the "Clear History" button inside the History popup to erase your deleted progress log.
  • Clear notifications: Use the "Clear All" button in the Notifications popup to remove all in-app notifications.
  • Clear search history: Use the "Clear All" option in the Quick Search popup to remove recent searches.
  • Opt out: Simply stop using the application. Your data remains in your browser's localStorage until you clear it.
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are always in full control. There is no account to deactivate and no server-side data deletion request needed.
Section 08
Children's Privacy

Progress Tracker is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Because the application does not require account registration or collect PII, there is minimal risk of inadvertent data collection from minors. However, we recommend that parents and guardians supervise their children's use of any web application.

If you believe a child under 13 has somehow provided personal information through a feedback or support form associated with this application, please contact us immediately and we will take appropriate steps to remove such information.
